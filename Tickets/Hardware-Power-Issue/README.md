# Ticket: PC Not Powering On (Hardware Support)

## 🧾 Ticket Information
- Ticket ID: 1002
- Priority: High
- Status: Resolved
- Category: Hardware
- Environment: Office Workstation (Trax System)

---

## 🧾 Scenario
A user submitted a high-priority ticket reporting that their computer was not working. The monitor displayed no signal, and the system appeared to be completely unresponsive.

---

## 🎯 Objective
Diagnose and resolve the issue preventing the workstation from powering on and restore normal operation.

---

## 🛠 Tools Used
- Visual inspection
- Basic hardware troubleshooting
- Power source verification

---

## 🔍 Troubleshooting Steps

### 1. Initial Assessment
- Observed that the monitor was off
- No lights or signs of power from the system

### 2. Checked Power Connections
- Verified that the power cables were connected properly
- Inspected the power outlet and power strip

### 3. Identified Issue
- Found that the power switch on the outlet/power strip was turned off

### 4. Implemented Fix
- Turned the power switch back on

### 5. Verified System Functionality
- Confirmed that the monitor powered on
- Confirmed the computer booted successfully into the operating system

---

## ✅ Resolution
The issue was resolved by turning on the power switch connected to the workstation. The system and monitor powered on normally and resumed full functionality.

---

## ⏱ Time to Resolve
5 minutes
